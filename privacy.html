Privacy Policy

Family Wallet (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our support site or use our app.

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect:

  • Name – to know who to address.
  • Email address – so we can reply.
  • Message contents – your support request details.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to respond to your support requests. We do not share, sell, or otherwise distribute your personal information to any third parties.

Data Security

All submissions are transmitted over secure (HTTPS) connections and stored via Formspree’s secure infrastructure. We follow industry best practices to protect your data.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is designed for parents, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 13.
